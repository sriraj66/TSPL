Transforming Tamilnadu Cricket: The Rise of TSPL

Tamilnadu Street Premier League (TSPL) is a premier sports organization committed to advancing softball cricket throughout Tamilnadu. Founded by Tennis ball Cricket federation, TSPL aims to create a thriving platform for aspiring cricketers, providing avenues for skill development, competitive play, and community engagement. The association's flagship event, the Tamilnadu Street Premier League (TSPL), showcases top talent from across Tamilnadu, with 16 teams representing 36 districts. Known for its grand events, including franchise launches, celebrity-backed player auctions, and live telecasts, TSPL is dedicated to enriching Tamilnadu's cricket culture.
